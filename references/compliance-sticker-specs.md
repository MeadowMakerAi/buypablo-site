# PABLO Compliance Sticker Specifications — NY Adult-Use Cannabis

## Overview

Every PABLO product unit gets stickers applied per batch. Stickers are batch-variable because THC/CBD percentages, lot numbers, expiration dates, and rotating warnings differ per batch.

Applicable regulation: NY OCM Part 128 (Cannabis Regulations), specifically §128.5.

## Sticker 1: Front Potency Sticker

**Dimensions:** Approximately 40mm wide × 12mm tall (adjust to fit existing THC/CBD text area on front panel)

**Content (exact text):**
```
THC: XX.X% · CBD: X.X%
```

**Typography:**
- Font: Arial or Helvetica, bold
- Size: 7-8pt (must be ≥6pt per §128.5(c))
- Color: black text on white background
- Contrast: high — white sticker on blue bag

**Notes:**
- XX.X% values are filled per batch from the COA
- This satisfies the PPDP requirement for Total THC and Total CBD as percentage of weight (§128.5(a)(2))
- The "NET WT: 1G" is already printed on the bag and does not need to be on the sticker (it's static per SKU)

## Sticker 2: Back Compliance Sticker

**Dimensions:** Approximately 50mm wide × 55mm tall (may need to go larger depending on how the text fits at 6pt minimum — size the zone accordingly)

**Content (exact text, top to bottom):**

ALL text below is in Arial, minimum 6pt, black on white.

### SECTION 1 — ROTATING WARNING (use ONE per batch)

Three versions of this sticker will be printed. Each batch gets one. Across the calendar year, each version should appear on approximately equal numbers of units.

**Version A:**
> WARNING: Cannabis can be addictive.

**Version B:**
> WARNING: Cannabis can impair concentration and coordination. Do not operate a vehicle or machinery under the influence of cannabis.

**Version C:**
> WARNING: There may be health risks associated with consumption of this product.

### SECTION 2 — FIXED WARNINGS (same on every sticker)

> Warning: Do not use if pregnant or nursing.
> Poison Center 1-800-222-1222
> Warning: Smoking or vaping is hazardous to health.

### SECTION 3 — PROCESSOR INFORMATION

```
info@buypablo.com
```

### SECTION 4 — INGREDIENTS

```
Ingredients: Cannabis flower, cannabis concentrate, cannabis terpenes
```

### SECTION 5 — BATCH-VARIABLE DATA

```
THC: XX.X% | CBD: X.X%
LOT: [lot number]

```

### SECTION 6 — QR CODE + TERPENE PROFILE

- QR code links to downloadable Certificate of Analysis per §128.5(b)(9)
- Terpene profile required because PABLO markets terpenes — §128.5(b)(10)
- Terpene values from COA

```
[QR CODE] TERPENE PROFILE:
Scan for COA    [Terpene 1]: X.X%
                [Terpene 2]: X.X%
                [Terpene 3]: X.X%
```

## Typography Rules (mandatory — §128.5(h))

- Font: Arial or Helvetica ONLY (Times New Roman and Calibri also acceptable)
- Size: minimum 6pt throughout — no exceptions
- Color: black text on white sticker
- Warning text: the word "WARNING:" or "Warning:" in bold, rest in regular weight
- All text in English
- Must contrast from background (black on white satisfies this)

## Layout Notes

- Sections separated by thin hairline rules or 2pt spacing
- QR code minimum ~12mm × 12mm for reliable scanning
- Terpene profile can sit beside the QR code to save vertical space
- If the sticker exceeds 55mm height, either increase the sticker zone on the bag or reduce to the minimum 6pt throughout
