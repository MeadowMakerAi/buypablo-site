## Pricing
- Undercut MFNY on live resin, undercut Jaunty/Ayrloom on distillate
- Processor gross margin ≥30% (target 35%+), retailer markup ≥2.0× (target 2.2×)
- MFNY benchmark: 510 LR 0.5g WSP $22.50, AIO LR 0.5g WSP $24.00. Consistent 50% margin to dispensary
- PABLO 510 LR 0.5g estimated COGS ~$8.17 (oil $5 + hardware $1.75 + mylar $0.22 + labor $1 + testing $0.20). At $18 WSP → ~50% gross margin

## Marketing
- Per-unit brand investment levy ($0.25–$0.50/unit) below gross profit in management entity P&L (NOT COGS — 280E)
- ~60% budtender/dispensary relationships; ~40% brand presence (IG organic, buypablo.com, menu accuracy)
- AVOID: SEO agencies, paid digital, influencers, trade show booths

## Competitive Landscape
- **Jaunty:** Market leader, 47,798 units, 67.4% distribution
- **MFNY:** #2, 63,773 510 carts. Ships mylars in ziplocks with METRC tag
- **Fernway:** 101,822 disposable units / 90 days
- **Whitespace:** Affordable live resin — virtually no brand below $45 retail for 0.5g
- **Targets:** FlynnStoned (~13 locations), Curaleaf, RISE, Verilife, Just a Little Higher, The Travel Agency
